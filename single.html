Let's GIT personal

I've spent the last 5 years in the transportation industry. I've done rideshare as well as para-transit for the elderly and people with disabilities. Being a professional driver taught me the importance of always being prepared and how an eye for detail can work in your favor. I've learned to be considerate of other peoples' time and pride myself in being a very prompt person.

Aside from coding, I love music and love to travel! Music has been a passion of mine since a child. I enjoy several different genres of music and really appreciate a good musical performance. You can always find me at a concert or somewhere around Chicago enjoying some live music. From artist showcases, to dinner and live music at a lounge, to out of town music festivals, I make sure to get my regular dose of musical medicine!

International travel is a newfound hobby of mine that I've grown to love because you get to see some beautiful places and learn about cultures completely different from your own. Some of the most interesting places I've visited recently are Ghana, Thailand, and Jamaica. Visiting an elephant sanctuary and taking a 4 hour cooking class while in Thailand really gave me a solid feel for the culture and allowed me to connect directly with locals. Travel is my new favorite way to create everlasting memories.
